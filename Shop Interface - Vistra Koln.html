Koln's Forge
Vistra Koln · Smith & Armorer
The hammer hasn't stopped since you crossed the threshold. A dwarf woman stands at the anvil, grey-streaked hair pulled back, arms like ship cable, talking to a glowing strip of metal in full sentences. She hasn't looked at you yet. She will when she's finished negotiating with the iron. "You don't need plate. You can't afford plate. And you'd fall over in plate."
