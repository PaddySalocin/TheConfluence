Sire's
Pellam Sire · Apothecary & Esoterica
A converted house at the village edge, the garden long since rioted past its fences. Inside: shelves of colored bottles, bundles of dried herbs, stacked scrolls, and a half-elf who looks like he hasn't slept since the last moon but knows exactly where everything is. He'll sell you what you need. He'll also tell you something you didn't ask for. He will not explain.
